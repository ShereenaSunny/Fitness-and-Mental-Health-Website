Ketogenic Diet

  • It involves reducing carbohydrate intake and upping fat intake. It sounds contrary to common sense, but it allows the body to burn fat as a fuel, rather than carbohydrates.

What you should include in it:

Healthy fats, such as those in avocados, coconuts, Brazil nuts, seeds, oily fish, and olive oil are liberally added to the diet to maintain an overall emphasis on fat.The diet causes the break down of fat deposits for fuel and creates substances called ketones through a process called ketosis.
